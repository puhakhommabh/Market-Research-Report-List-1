<p><p>The laparotomy sponges market is segmented into three main types: radiopaque laparotomy sponges, traditional laparotomy sponges, and RFID or barcode laparotomy sponges. Radiopaque sponges contain materials that are visible on X-rays, helping in the detection of retained sponges post-surgery. Traditional sponges are standard absorbent products for surgical procedures. RFID or barcode lapartomy sponges incorporate tracking technology, allowing for improved inventory management and reducing the risk of retained sponges, enhancing surgical safety and efficiency.</p></p>
