<p><p>The Thin Layer Deposition Equipment market includes various techniques for depositing thin films. Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) utilizes physical processes like evaporation and sputtering to create thin layers. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) involves chemical reactions to produce solid materials on substrates, offering uniform coverage and high-quality films. Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) provides atomic-scale control over film thickness through sequential self-limiting reactions, ensuring precision in advanced applications like semiconductors and nanotechnology. Each method serves distinct market needs and applications.</p></p>
