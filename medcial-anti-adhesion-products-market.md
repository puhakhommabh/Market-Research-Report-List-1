<p><p>The medical anti-adhesion products market includes two primary types: anti-adhesion films and anti-adhesion gels. Anti-adhesion films are thin layers of material applied to surgical sites to prevent tissue sticking during the healing process, reducing complications. In contrast, anti-adhesion gels are viscous substances that create a protective barrier between tissues. Both products aim to minimize postoperative adhesions, enhance patient recovery, and improve surgical outcomes, driving their demand in various surgical specialties and contributing to advances in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
