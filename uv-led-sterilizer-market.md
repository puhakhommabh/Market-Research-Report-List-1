<p><p>The UV LED sterilizer market is categorized into three main types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs are primarily used for curing applications and minimal sterilization. UV-B LEDs have a role in medical therapies and disinfection but are less common in sterilization technologies. UV-C LEDs are the most effective for germicidal applications, targeting bacteria and viruses with short wavelengths, making them ideal for sterilization in healthcare, food safety, and water treatment sectors.</p></p>
