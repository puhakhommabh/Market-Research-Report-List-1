<p><p>The metal fibers market encompasses various types of production methods. Vacuum deposition involves evaporating metal in a vacuum to form thin films. Coating metal powder with binders combines fine metal particles with adhesive materials for shaping. Sputter coating uses high-energy particles to deposit metal onto surfaces, creating durable coatings. Electroless coating relies on chemical reactions to deposit a metal layer without electric current, providing uniform coverage. Each method serves specific applications in industries like electronics, automotive, and textiles.</p></p>
