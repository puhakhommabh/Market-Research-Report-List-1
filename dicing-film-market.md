<p><p>The dicing film market is categorized into non-conductive and conductive types, each serving distinct purposes in semiconductor manufacturing. Non-conductive dicing films are primarily used for delicate materials, providing insulation and preventing electrical interference during the cutting process. In contrast, conductive dicing films facilitate the safe separation of conductive materials, ensuring efficient dissipation of static charges. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing yield and precision in the dicing process, catering to varying requirements of electronic device fabrication.</p></p>
