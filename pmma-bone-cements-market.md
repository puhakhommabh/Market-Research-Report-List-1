<p><strong>The PMMA Bone Cements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Cements</li><li>Medium Viscosity Cements</li><li>High Viscosity Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PMMA bone cements are categorized into three types based on their viscosity, which influences their application in orthopedic procedures. Low viscosity cements are designed for simple injection and flow easily into bone cavities, ideal for minimally invasive surgeries. Medium viscosity cements strike a balance, providing moderate flow and increased handling time, making them versatile for various implants. High viscosity cements offer superior mechanical strength and stability, suitable for load-bearing applications and providing excellent fixation in complex orthopedic scenarios.</p></p>
