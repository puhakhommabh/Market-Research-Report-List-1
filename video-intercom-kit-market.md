<p><p>The video intercom kit market comprises two main types: Analog Type and IP Type. In contrast, IP intercoms leverage internet protocol networks, allowing for advanced features such as video streaming, remote access, and integration with smart home systems. IP systems offer greater flexibility, scalability, and enhanced security, making them increasingly popular in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
