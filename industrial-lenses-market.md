<p><strong>The Industrial Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li><li>F-Mount</li><li>S-Mount</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lenses market includes various types based on mounting systems. C-Mount lenses are popular for CCTV and machine vision, offering compatibility with a wide range of cameras. CS-Mount lenses are similar but designed for shorter flange distances, allowing more compact camera installations. F-Mount lenses are primarily used in photography and industrial applications, while S-Mount lenses are smaller, catering to miniaturized cameras. Other types encompass specialized or less common lens mounts, broadening the market spectrum for specific use cases.</p></p>
