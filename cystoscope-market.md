<p><p>The cystoscope market is primarily divided into rigid and flexible cystoscopes. Rigid cystoscopes are characterized by a solid structure, offering high durability and a clear visual field, making them suitable for surgical interventions. In contrast, flexible cystoscopes feature a more pliable design, allowing for easier navigation through the urethra and bladder, which enhances patient comfort during diagnostics.
