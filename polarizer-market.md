<p><ul><li>TFT Type</li><li>TN Type</li><li>STN Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polarizer market is categorized into three main types: TFT (Thin-Film Transistor), TN (Twisted Nematic), and STN (Super Twisted Nematic). TFT polarizers are commonly used in high-resolution displays due to their superior image quality and color reproduction. TN polarizers are cost-effective and widely used in basic applications, offering faster response times but poorer color performance. STN polarizers enhance the contrast and visibility of displays, making them suitable for applications requiring better image quality while still being cost-efficient.</p></p>
