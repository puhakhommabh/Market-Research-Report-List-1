<p><p>The Porous Calcium Polyphosphate Bioceramic market comprises two key types: biological inert ceramics and bioactive ceramics. Biological inert ceramics are designed to be biocompatible and stable within biological environments, providing structural support without actively participating in biological processes. In contrast, bioactive ceramics interact with surrounding tissues, fostering natural tissue regeneration and promoting integration with bone. Both types are utilized in various medical applications, such as implants and scaffolds, to enhance healing and support bone growth.</p></p>
