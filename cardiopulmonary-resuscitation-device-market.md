<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) device market is divided into two main types: manual and automatic devices. Manual devices require trained personnel to apply chest compressions and rescue breaths, allowing for flexibility in technique and patient response. In contrast, automatic CPR devices operate with minimal human intervention, delivering consistent compressions at predefined rates and depths, which can enhance the efficiency of resuscitation efforts. Both types play crucial roles in emergency medical care, catering to different clinical settings and needs.</p></p>
