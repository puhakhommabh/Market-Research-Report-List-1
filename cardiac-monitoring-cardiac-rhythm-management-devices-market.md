<p><p>Cardiac monitoring and management devices encompass various technologies for diagnosing and treating heart conditions. ECG devices track electrical activity, detecting arrhythmias. Implantable loop recorders provide long-term monitoring of cardiac rhythms. Cardiac output monitoring measures the efficiency of heart function. Defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm in emergencies, while pacemakers regulate heartbeat in patients with irregular rhythms. Other devices may include wearable monitors and telemetry systems, enhancing heart health management through continuous observation and intervention.</p></p>
