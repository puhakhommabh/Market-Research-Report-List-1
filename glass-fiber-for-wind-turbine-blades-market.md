<p><strong>The Glass Fiber for Wind Turbine Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand Lay-up</li><li>Prepreg</li><li>Vacuum Introduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber market for wind turbine blades encompasses several manufacturing processes: hand lay-up, prepreg, and vacuum infusion. Hand lay-up involves manually placing glass fiber layers in a mold and applying resin, offering flexibility but requiring skilled labor. Prepreg uses pre-impregnated fibers that are cured under heat and pressure, ensuring consistent quality and reduced labor. Vacuum infusion relies on a vacuum to draw resin through dry fibers, providing enhanced fiber-resin bonding and minimizing voids, resulting in stronger and lighter blades.</p></p>
