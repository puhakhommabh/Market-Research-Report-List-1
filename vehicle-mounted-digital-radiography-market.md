<p><p>The vehicle-mounted digital radiography market features two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR) systems. CR tech utilizes imaging plates to capture and store data, which is then processed digitally, offering portability and flexibility in various environments. In contrast, DR tech employs direct digital detectors for immediate image capture and processing, enhancing efficiency and image quality. Both systems are designed for use in emergency and remote locations, providing crucial diagnostic capabilities on-the-go.</p></p>
