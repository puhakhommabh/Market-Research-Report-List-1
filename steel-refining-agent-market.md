<p><p>The steel refining agent market comprises three primary types: hybrid, premelted, and sintered. Hybrid agents combine multiple materials to enhance efficiency and reduce impurities during the steelmaking process. Premelted agents are pre-treated to ensure uniformity and better performance, facilitating smoother refining operations. Sintered agents are produced through a process that fuses small particles, resulting in a concentrated form that can improve the stability of additives in steel production. Each type offers distinct advantages based on production needs.</p></p>
