<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-Ray Imaging</li><li>Extraoral X-Ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray system market encompasses two primary types: intraoral and extraoral X-ray imaging. Intraoral X-ray imaging involves capturing images from within the mouth, allowing for detailed views of teeth and surrounding structures, which aids in diagnosing cavities, infections, and other dental issues. Extraoral X-ray imaging, on the other hand, captures images from outside the mouth, encompassing broader views of the jaw and skull, essential for orthodontic assessments and treatment planning. Both types are crucial for comprehensive dental care.</p></p>
