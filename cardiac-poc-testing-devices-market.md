<p><p>Cardiac point-of-care (POC) testing devices encompass various market types, primarily including troponin tests, brain natriuretic peptide (BNP) tests, and other tests. Troponin tests are essential for diagnosing myocardial infarction by measuring cardiac-specific proteins. BNP tests help assess heart failure by quantifying levels of the peptide linked to cardiac stress. The "others" category includes tests for cardiac enzymes, markers for ischemia, and additional indicators of heart function, thereby expanding diagnostic capabilities in critical and emergency care settings.</p></p>
