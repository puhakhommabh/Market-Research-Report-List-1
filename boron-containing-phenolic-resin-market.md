<p><strong>The Boron Containing Phenolic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addition Reaction</li><li>Condensation Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boron Containing Phenolic Resin market is primarily categorized into two types based on their chemical formation: addition and condensation reactions. Addition reaction resins are synthesized by incorporating boron into the phenolic structure through a straightforward reaction, leading to enhanced thermal and mechanical properties. In contrast, condensation reaction resins are formed through the reaction of phenolic compounds with boron-containing agents, resulting in a more complex network that typically exhibits improved chemical resistance and performance in high-temperature applications.</p></p>
