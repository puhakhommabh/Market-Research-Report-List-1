<p><p>The medical mechanical ventilators market comprises two primary types: non-invasive and invasive ventilators. Non-invasive ventilators support patients without requiring intubation, utilizing masks or nasal interfaces to provide respiratory assistance, ideal for conditions like obstructive sleep apnea. Invasive ventilators, on the other hand, involve the insertion of a tube into the patient's airway, offering direct support for severe respiratory failure or during surgery. Both types are crucial in critical care, enhancing patient outcomes by ensuring adequate oxygenation and ventilation.</p></p>
