<p><strong>The Intelligent Ion Chromatograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Ion Chromatograph market is segmented into three main types: Ion Exchange Chromatography, Ion Exclusion Chromatography, and Ion Pair Chromatography. Ion Exchange Chromatography separates ions based on their charge, primarily used for analyzing cations and anions. Ion Exclusion Chromatography focuses on separating ionic and non-ionic species, making it suitable for organic acids and sugars. Ion Pair Chromatography involves pairing ions with counterions to enhance separation in complex matrices, utilized widely in pharmaceuticals and environmental testing.</p></p>
