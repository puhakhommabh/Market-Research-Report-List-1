<p><p>The solenoid valve market is segmented into various types based on configuration. The 2-way valve allows flow control in one direction, typically used for on/off applications. The 3-way valve facilitates flow between two outlets, enabling mixing or diverting functions. The 4-way valve, often used in pneumatic systems, directs airflow and controls cylinders with more complexity. The 5-way valve, featuring multiple ports, offers advanced control options for multi-actuator systems. Each type serves specific applications in automation and fluid control.</p></p>
