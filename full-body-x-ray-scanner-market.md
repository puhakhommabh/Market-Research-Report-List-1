<p><strong>The Full Body X-ray Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Backscatter X-ray Scanner</li><li>Through-body X-ray Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Body X-ray Scanner market includes two primary types: Backscatter X-ray Scanners and Through-body X-ray Scanners. Backscatter X-ray Scanners utilize low-energy X-rays to reveal concealed objects by detecting scattered radiation reflecting off surfaces, making them effective for security checkpoints. In contrast, Through-body X-ray Scanners emit higher energy X-rays that penetrate the body to produce detailed images of internal structures. Both technologies enhance security in airports, government buildings, and public spaces by detecting concealed weapons and contraband.</p></p>
