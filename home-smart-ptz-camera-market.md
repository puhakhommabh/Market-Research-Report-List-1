<p><p>The Home Smart PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) camera market consists of two primary types: fixed and electric. In contrast, electric type cameras offer advanced features, allowing users to remotely pan, tilt, and zoom to monitor various areas dynamically. This flexibility makes electric type cameras more suitable for comprehensive surveillance needs, whereas fixed types are often used for simpler monitoring setups.</p></p>
