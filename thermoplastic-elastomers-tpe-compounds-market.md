<p><p>Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) compounds are categorized primarily into three types. Styrenic Block Copolymers (TPE-S) combine the properties of rubber and thermoplastics, offering flexibility and processability. Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV) feature a dynamic vulcanization process, providing superior heat and chemical resistance while maintaining elasticity. Other TPE types include polyurethane-based and polyolefin-based elastomers, each catering to specific applications in automotive, medical, and consumer goods sectors due to their unique performance characteristics and versatility in processing.</p></p>
