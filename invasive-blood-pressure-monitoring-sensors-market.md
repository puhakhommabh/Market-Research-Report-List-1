<p><p>Invasive blood pressure monitoring sensors are categorized into single, dual, and triple channel sensors based on their capability to measure pressures in various locations simultaneously. Single channel sensors focus on monitoring one vascular site, providing essential data for critical care. Dual channel sensors enhance this by enabling concurrent measurements from two sites, improving patient monitoring. Triple channel sensors further expand capabilities, allowing real-time tracking from three different sites, facilitating comprehensive hemodynamic assessment in complex clinical situations.</p></p>
