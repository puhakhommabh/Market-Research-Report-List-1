<p><ul><li>Bioanalyses</li><li>Chemical Analyses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biodetection market comprises two main types: bioanalyses and chemical analyses. Bioanalyses focus on detecting biological entities, such as pathogens or biomarkers, using methods like immunoassays, nucleic acid tests, and biosensors. These techniques are essential for medical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety. On the other hand, chemical analyses involve the identification and quantification of chemical substances, employing techniques such as chromatography, spectroscopy, and mass spectrometry. Both segments play critical roles in various industries, including healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture.</p></p>
