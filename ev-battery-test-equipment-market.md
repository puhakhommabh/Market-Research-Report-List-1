<p><p>The EV battery test equipment market can be categorized into three main levels: pack level, module level, and cell level. Pack level testing evaluates the entire battery system's performance and safety, ensuring it meets vehicle requirements. Module level testing examines individual battery modules, assessing their efficiency and reliability. Cell level testing focuses on individual cells within the modules, measuring their voltage, capacity, and cycle life. Each level plays a crucial role in ensuring the overall performance and safety of electric vehicles.</p></p>
